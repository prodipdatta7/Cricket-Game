Welcome to the Cricket Game website
Rules:
This is a simple cricket game based on random event. The rules are as below:
  • Choose two teams. The first one is your team and the second one is your opponent team.
  • Choose how many overs match it gonna be. There are 3 options currently available.
    1. 2 overs match.
    2. 5 overs match.
    3. 10 overs match.
  • Time to checkout the team players.
    1. If it's a 2 overs match then every team will consists of 5 players
    2. If it's a 5 overs match then every team will consists of 7 players
    3. If it's a 10 overs match then every team will consists of 11 players
  • For the toss choose one of the options of Head or Tail.
  • Now spin the coin.
  • If you won the toss then choose whether you want bowl to first or bat.
  • If opponent own the toss then they will choose whether they want to bowl first or bat.
  • If you are the bowling team, press a button called BOWL to bowled a ball.
  • If you are batting team, press a button called HIT to face a ball.
  • In the live-commentary section you will see the description of every ball bowled by the bowler.
  • In the scorecard tab you will see scorecard of the game.
  • For every ball bowled you will see one of the verdict of:
    • [0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 6]: The number indicates how many runs were made in a particular ball.
    • W: Fall of a wicket.
    • Wd: Wide delivery.
    • N: No ball. The next ball will be a free hit.
    • 2Wd: 2 runs were made in a wide delivery.
    • 3Wd: 3 runs were made in a wide delivery.
    • 4Wd: 4 runs were made in a wide delivery.
    • 5Wd: 5 runs were made in a wide delivery.
    • 1N: Extra 1 runs were made in a No ball delivery. total run scored: 2
    • 2N: Extra 2 runs were made in a No ball delivery. total run scored: 3
    • 3N: Extra 3 runs were made in a No ball delivery. total run scored: 4
    • 4N: Extra 4 runs were made in a No ball delivery. total run scored: 5
    • 6N: Extra 6 runs were made in a No ball delivery. total run scored: 7
